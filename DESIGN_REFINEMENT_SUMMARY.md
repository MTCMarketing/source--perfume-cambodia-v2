# Cambodian-Inspired Luxury Design Refinement

## Overview
Successfully refined the fragrance website design to subtly reflect the Cambodian market while maintaining a modern, premium aesthetic. The heavy dark blue (#121C4F) has been replaced with softer, more refined colors, and subtle Cambodian cultural elements have been tastefully integrated.

## Key Changes Made

- **Replaced heavy dark blue** (#121C4F) with refined navy (#2C3E50)

## Cultural Integration Strategy
The Cambodian elements are implemented with extreme subtlety:
- **5% opacity gradients** to avoid overwhelming the luxury aesthetic
- **Thin accent lines** (1-2px) for delicate cultural references
- **Hover effects** that reveal cultural colors without being intrusive
- **Flag colors used sparingly** in gradients and transitions only

## Result
The website now feels culturally aligned with the Cambodian market while maintaining its premium fragrance brand identity. The design is sophisticated, modern, and subtly honors the local culture without compromising the luxury aesthetic.
